References

There are many books about the UP. There are also some online process resources (collection of web pages, templates, and samples):

Rational Unified Process
sold by IBM. IBM gives KU a license under the Academic Initiative program, so you can download it for free. An older version is installed on se.cpe.ku.ac.th.
Open UP
free process material developed by the Eclipse Process Framework (EPF) project, at epf.eclipse.org (but hasn't been updated). You can download it there. Also installed on se.cpe.ku.ac.th.
