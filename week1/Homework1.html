Homework 1

You will obtain background info about a POS for small business by interviewing someone from a small business. You can do this alone or ''work with one other person''. If you work with a partner, submit one copy of your results with both your names.

1. Assignment for Business using POS

  1. What is the name and location of the business? What is the name of person you interviewed?
  2. What is the nature of the business?
  3. What functions are performed by their POS? (for example, making sales, manage inventory, making refunds, compute total sales for each day)
  4. How does he/she use the POS to make a sale?
  5. If the owner agrees, take a photo (or screendump) of POS. Include photo in your report and annotate it to describe what is on the screen.
  6. What external systems or hardware does POS use? For example, does it perform credit card verifcation, connect to printer, connect to back-end system for product info?
  7. What does the business feel are pros and cons of their POS?
  8. What features would they like to have or see improved in a POS?
  9. Include other relevant questions you think of.

2. Assignment for Business Not using POS

As a researcher, you are looking for likely opportunities for a POS. Some small businesses are very unlikely to want a POS because it doesn't add value and doesn't fit into their "work flow". For example, a laundary, a barber shop (unless there are several employees), fish or vegetable sellers at a flea market. Avoid unpromising businesses.

For a business not using a POS, using a mock-up of your ideas (or get them to help draw a mock-up) might help. They may not see any benefit of a POS until they have a visual idea of what a POS might do. But, you don't want to bias their thinking so don't show your own mockup right away. ''Seek first to understand''.

  1. What is the name and location of the business? What is the name of person you interviewed?
  2. What is the nature of the business?
  3. If the owner agrees to specify, what is their approximate weekly (or monthly) sales total?
  4. What is the reason for not using a POS?
  5. How to they perform sales and compute prices?
  6. This question will help identify opportunity for our POS:
    If there was a free mobile POS that runs on smart phone or tablet computer, would he/she consider using it in their business?
  7. If "yes" to above, what important feature or characteristic should a POS have to make them want to adopt it?
  8. If "no" to above, why do they think its not useful?
  9. Include any other questions you ask.

Report

Write a short report for both businesses, on separate papers.

  1. At the top of each report, write the business name, location, name of person you spoke to, and description of the business.
  2. Summarize the answers to the questions you asked.
  3. For business using a POS, include a photo or screenshot of their POS. Please annotate the image to show what the components on the screen do and how to make a sale.
  4. For the business not using a POS, if they thought your mock-up was promising and gave feedback, include your mock-up in report.
  5. What is your conclusion? Does this business represent an opportunity for a mobile POS? Why or why not? What are the important factors? What are the barriers, competition, or threats to writing a POS for this kind of business?
